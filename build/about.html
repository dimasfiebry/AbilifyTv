HISTORY

Ability For You (Abilify) has evolved in response to the social challenges faced by individuals with disabilities, especially the deaf and mute, in dealing with social isolation and low self-confidence. Recognizing that stigma and communication barriers can impede their well-being, Abilify introduces an inclusive platform called Ability For You. Launched with the primary goal of creating a friendly and supportive online environment, Abilify provides a space for the community to share stories, experiences, and inspiration.
